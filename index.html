Our Team Goal

Triple Helix strives to inspire the next generation of innovators, to teach others gracious professionalism, and to push ourselves to do our best. We can connect with more people and lead them to do the same.

Learn Valuable Skills

Ever wanted to use a milling machine or solder? Learn new skills from experienced mentors by participating directly in a comfortable, accepting work environment.

Gain Unique Experiences

Compete in various competitions, from local to international, and show new skills while cheering loudly for the team. Meet new friends and influential people from around the globe.
